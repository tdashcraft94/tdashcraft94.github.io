Who Are We?

The South Valley Plant Collective is a community group dedicated to educating, sharing, and enjoying all things plant related! We specifically focus on indoor plants as well as personal outdoor gardening. We welcome everyone into our group, whether you have a certified green thumb, or you are just trying to keep your first plant alive.
